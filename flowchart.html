BitWarranty Cross-Functional Flow Chart

This flow chart illustrates the end-to-end process for a BitWarranty event, including interactions between different environments, users, channels, and entities.

Consumer

Purchase Product/Service
Register Warranty via App
Access Warranty Details
Submit Warranty Claim
Receive Claim Status

BitWarranty Platform

Receive Warranty Registration
Store Warranty Data on Blockchain
Provide Warranty Details to Consumer
Process Warranty Claim

Seller

Create Warranty Template
Register Warranty on BitWarranty
Validate Warranty Claims
Approve/Reject Claims
Provide Feedback on Claims

Blockchain Network

Store Warranty Registration
Validate Data Integrity
Log Warranty Transactions
Provide Tamper-Proof History
Facilitate Secure Transactions

Customer Support

Assist with Warranty Registration
Answer Consumer Queries

Resolve Disputes
